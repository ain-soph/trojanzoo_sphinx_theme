Installation¶

Via Git or Download¶

Symlink or subtree the trojanzoo_sphinx_theme repository into your documentation at docs/_themes/trojanzoo_sphinx_theme then add the following two settings to your Sphinx conf.py file:

html_theme = "trojanzoo_sphinx_theme"
html_theme_path = ["_themes", ]
